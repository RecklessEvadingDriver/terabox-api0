WHAT TO LOOK FOR IN LOGS:

✓ Good Signs:
  "🚀 Proxy Pool Manager starting..."
  "✅ Proxy pool ready: X alive proxies"
  "✅ Startup done!"

⚠ Warnings (OK to ignore):
  "Proxy pool startup error... continuing with empty pool"
  "No alive proxies! Direct connection use ho raha hai"

✗ Bad Signs (would indicate issues):
  "Failed to import main"
  "AttributeError" or "NameError"
  Timeout errors during cold start
